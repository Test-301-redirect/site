Introduction to CrowdStarter

What is CrowdStarter?

CrowdStarter is an all-in-one innovative next-gen fundraising platform, aimed at creating a central space on which fundraisers, individuals, businesses and start-ups can bring ideas and products to market, build brand awareness and gain early adopters and loyal advocates.

What can you do on CrowdStarter?

Launch fundraising campaigns

CrowdStarter enables you to set-up and launch fundraisers as an individual, business or organisation.

Collect funds securely

Raise funds online in a secure environment that uses state-of-art payment technology and supports all forms of online payment including all credit/debit cards, EFT and much more.

Offer rewards to contributors

CrowdStarter is the world's first crowdfunding platform to enable both donation and reward based fundraisers. Reward tracking tools included!

Manage your campaign

Promote your campaign, monitor funds, track analytics, stay updated on comments, post updates and much more on your own personal campaign dashboard.

Engagement

Engage with your audience by using our post-update and commenting tools. Contributors can leave comments and engage with others on your campaign page, increasing social proof.

Turn ideas into reality

Bring ideas and products to market with a well-rounded campaign. 

CrowdStarter provides you with the tools to share your campaign with the world.

And so much more!

What does all this cost?

Nothing. Starting and launching your campaign is totally free. Our support team is also available to provide you with campaign guidance and advice 7-days a week at no charge.  

Fees. Platform and transaction fees are only charged on funds raised. So you have nothing to lose and everything to gain by launching a campaign.

Platform fees range from 3 - 5%. Learn more about platform fees, transaction fees and try out our online goal calculator here.
